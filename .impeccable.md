## Design Context

### Users
- **Primary Audience:** Peers, recruiters, and technical readers.
- **Goal:** To showcase engineering rigor, personality, and contributions through a distraction-free reader experience.
- **Context:** Looking for information on your skills, experience, and deep technical learning.

### Brand Personality
- **Technical:** Rigorous and well-engineered.
- **Minimal:** Clean, focused, and free of clutter.
- **Friendly:** Warm, approachable, and human (not "corporate cold").

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme:** Catppuccin Mocha (Dark) and Latte (Light) palettes.
- **Typography:** Aleo (Serif) for readability and warmth, Montserrat (Sans) for bold decorative headers, IBM Plex Mono for technical accuracy.
- **References:** Inspired by Mitchell Hashimoto's blog (minimalism, speed, readability).
- **Feel:** Reader-friendly, distraction-less, and warm.

### Design Principles
1. **Content First:** Navigation and metadata should support, never compete with, the core reading experience.
2. **Technical Warmth:** Use the "soft" Catppuccin palette to make technical information feel approachable.
3. **Subtle Rigor:** Focus on alignment, precise spacing, and refined typography instead of flashy animations.
4. **Adaptive Simplicity:** Ensure the layout remains elegant even as new sections (Reading, Projects) are added.
